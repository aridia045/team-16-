Grandma's Listening, Dear!

Hello there, my dear! Come on in and tell Grandma what's on your mind today. Are you studying hard, or do you just need a friendly ear?

This is the About section for "I Miss My Grandma's House" web app, a project for a hackathon. It aims to provide a comforting and supportive environment for users, featuring a grandma-like chatbot for study support and emotional venting, along with helpful tools like a Pomodoro timer.
